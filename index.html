Welcome to the Philosophy Hub!

Welcome to Philosophy Hub, young pupils! Prepare to take a journey through the many eras of philosophy and become enlightened. Under the "Learn More" tab, you can choose an era to explore and learn about each of its more influential philosophers. Click on the "Philosophical Era Quiz", answer a few questions, and discover which philosophical era you are! Have fun, and remember: as a rule, not knowing is a step towards new knowledge!

All information on this website is based on the novel Sophie's World by Jostein Gaarder
