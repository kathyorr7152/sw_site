Antiquity

Socrates

469-299 BCE

Rationalist

(one who has an unshakable faith in human reason)

Main Beliefs

Believed everyone had equal common

Believed in the importance of discussion

Philosophical Project

Discovering what was innate and what was socially induced

Concerned with man and his place in society

Plato

427-347 BCE

Dualist

(one who effets sharp division between thought and extended reality)

Main Beliefs

Divided reality into two regions- the world of ideas and the world of senses

Believed everything tangible in nature flows

Believed soul existed before it inhabited the body

Philosophical Project

The study of nature and what it consists of

Aristotle

384-322 BCE

Empiricist

(believes everything in the mind comes from the senses)

Main Beliefs

Believed nature should be studied with senses, not reason

Believed we use our senses to create a “form” (disagreed with Plato’s “idea” world)

Believed everything in nature had the potential to realize or reach it’s form

Philosophical Project

The study and classification of nature
